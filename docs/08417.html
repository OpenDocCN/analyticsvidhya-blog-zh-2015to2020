  • actor _ 1 _ facebook _ 赞:70000
  • actor _ 2 _ facebook _ 赞:40000
  • actor _ 3 _ facebook _ 赞:50000

  • actor _ 1 _ facebook _ 赞:70000
  • actor _ 2 _ facebook _ 赞:40000
  • actor _ 3 _ facebook _ 赞:30000

因为在这种情况下，actor_3_facebook_likes是 30000，不到actor_1_facebook_likes的一半
