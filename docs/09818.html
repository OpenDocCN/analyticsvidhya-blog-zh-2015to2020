要运行此程序:

  1. 从:(https://www.npmjs.com/package/@zappar/zapworks-cli)下载zapworks cli
  2. 转到命令行(从下载的文件夹)，然后键入:zapworks serve .
  3. 转到 https://127.0.0.1:8080
